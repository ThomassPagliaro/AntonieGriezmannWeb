Family and Wife

Griezmann's father, Alain, was a town councilor and his mother, Isabelle, worked as a hospital cleaner. He has two siblings, an older sister named Maud and a younger brother named Theo, both of whom are also football players. Griezmann is married to Erika Choperena, whom he met when she was studying in San Sebastian, Spain. They got married in 2017, and the couple has two children together, a daughter named Mia and a son named Amaro.
